Pricing questions

Good to know

Do I bring my own API keys?
Yes. You connect your own AI (Claude, DeepSeek, etc.) and Ahrefs keys once at the workspace level. They're shared across all your sites, so you control and pay for usage directly.
What counts as a "workspace"?
One website with its own WordPress connection, Search Console property and isolated content store. The Starter plan includes one; Growth and Agency add more.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There's no lock-in — your content lives on your own WordPress site, and you can export or disconnect whenever you like.
Is there a free trial of the paid features?
The Starter plan is free forever for a single site. You can try autopilot, rank tracking and multi-site features on a Growth trial — start an account and upgrade in a click.
