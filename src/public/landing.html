Questions

Everything you might be wondering

Do I need an Ahrefs or AI subscription?
You connect your own API keys for the AI writer and Ahrefs once, at the workspace level. Those are shared across every site you run, so you pay for the power a single time and use it everywhere.
Will the content read like generic AI?
No. Every article is built to Rank Math standards with tuned keyword density (1–1.1%), readability checks, schema, FAQs, internal links and rich on-brand presentation blocks — then surfaced for your approval before it goes live.
How does multi-tenancy keep my clients separate?
Each website owner gets their own isolated workspace with a separate data store. WordPress and Search Console are connected per tenant, while the AI writers and Ahrefs data are shared. One owner can never see another's content.
Is publishing to WordPress safe?
Yes. You connect using a WordPress Application Password with the exact rights you choose, and you can revoke it any time. Nothing is published you haven't approved — unless you explicitly turn on full autopilot.
Who becomes the admin?
The first person to register becomes the owner/super-admin and controls the shared settings. Everyone who registers after that gets their own workspace to connect their own site.
Can I try it before committing?
Absolutely — create your account, connect a site and generate your first articles. You can run a single site for free and scale up when you're ready.
